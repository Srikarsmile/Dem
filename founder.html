Our Founder

Meet Malcolm Gill

Malcolm Gill

Founder & CEO

"I feel so privileged and proud for the opportunity to bring this charity to you, as it is my passion and born from my personal experience of 11 years living with Dementia."

Malcolm founded Dignitate after caring for his mother Elizabeth for 11 years following her diagnosis with vascular dementia. His personal journey through the challenges of dementia care within the South Asian community inspired him to create an organization that provides culturally sensitive support to others facing similar challenges.

Roles & Expertise

Sought-After Speaker

Malcolm is regularly invited to speak at conferences and events about dementia care, cultural competence, and the importance of community support.

Cultural Competence Educator

Facilitates workshops on culturally sensitive dementia care, helping healthcare providers and organizations better serve diverse communities.

Advocate

Works tirelessly to bridge gaps in dementia care with cultural context, advocating for policy changes and improved services for South Asian communities.

Recognized Leader

Recognized for influencing policy, practice, and public perception around dementia care in cultural communities.
