Our Core Objectives

01

Raise Awareness

Promote early diagnosis and symptom recognition within South Asian communities, breaking down cultural barriers to seeking help.

02

Educate

Offer culturally appropriate training and resources for effective caregiving, tailored to the unique needs of our community.

03

Build Community

Create inclusive platforms that reduce stigma and foster connection among carers and families in Southeast Asian communities.

04

Support

Provide peer support from experienced caregivers who understand the cultural context and challenges you face.

Our Mission

To empower cultural carers through community networks, culturally competent support, and preventative care. We believe that no one should face dementia alone, and that culturally sensitive support can transform the caregiving experience.

Our Vision

To create environments where cultural carers receive full support, recognition, and equitable access to resources. We envision a world where dementia is understood, stigma is eliminated, and every family has the support they need.
